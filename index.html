Welcome Word

You are most warmly welcome in my website for a rapid preview over my work, my specialty and my publications in the field of orthopaedic surgery.

Prof Gamal Ahmed Hosny is

  • Emeritus Prof of orthopaedic surgery & X-Chairman of Orthopaedic Surgery Department, Benha University Hospitals.
  • X-Chairman of the permanent committee of Academic promotion to professorship in orthopaedic surgery in the Egyptian universities 2019 till 2022 & current board member.
  • Honorary Prof, Ilizarov Institute, Kurgan, Russia
  • X-President of Egyptian Orthopaedic Association.
  • X- President of International ASAMI and Bone reconstruction society
  • Co-Chairman of scientific committee of IFPOS.
  • Chairman of the pediatric orthopaedics subspeciality of SICOT.
  • X-President of ASAMI Egypt.
  • Clinical Fellow of EFORT
  • Specialty Editor of Journal of pediatric orthopaedics-B
  • President of IFPOS
  • President Elect of MEPOS
  • President of the 43rd SICOT World Orthopaedic congress 21-23 Nov 2023
